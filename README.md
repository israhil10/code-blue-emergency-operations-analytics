## 📌 Project Overview

**Code Blue – Emergency Operations & Patient Flow Analytics** is a three-page interactive Power BI dashboard developed for **FP20 Analytics Challenge #38**.

The project focuses on understanding emergency department performance, uncovering patient flow bottlenecks, evaluating workforce capacity, and analyzing healthcare outcomes.

By transforming healthcare data into actionable insights, this dashboard supports better operational decisions, improves patient throughput, and enhances overall care delivery.

# 🚑 Business Problem

Emergency departments operate under increasing pressure due to:

- Rising patient demand
- Capacity constraints
- Treatment delays
- Workforce burnout
- Resource allocation challenges

Without effective analytics, healthcare leaders struggle to identify bottlenecks and optimize patient throughput.

This project addresses these challenges through interactive dashboards that provide visibility into emergency operations, patient flow, workforce performance, and healthcare outcomes.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze emergency department activity.
- Identify patient flow bottlenecks.
- Evaluate waiting times and admission trends.
- Understand diagnosis and severity patterns.
- Monitor workforce utilization and burnout risks.
- Improve operational readiness and patient outcomes.

---

# 🏥 Page 1 – Emergency Operations Command Center

## Business Focus

Provide an executive overview of emergency department performance and patient outcomes.

### Key Insights

📈 **Demand Analysis**

- South East England contributed the highest ED demand.
- Regional differences in patient volume were identified.

🩺 **Diagnosis Patterns**

- Respiratory and Trauma/Injury categories generated the highest number of visits.
- Severity distribution revealed Moderate and High cases as the dominant segments.

🏥 **Patient Outcomes**

- Most patients were discharged successfully.
- Admission and readmission rates highlighted areas requiring operational attention.

# 🚨 Page 2 – Patient Flow & Bottleneck Analysis

## Business Focus

Identify delays and uncover bottlenecks affecting patient throughput.

### Key Insights

⏳ **Wait Time Performance**

- Average wait time remained around 61 minutes.
- Peak congestion periods were observed during specific hours.

🏥 **Admission Flow**

- Bed delays and longer stays indicated capacity constraints.
- Admission trends provided insights into patient movement.

🔥 **Delay Drivers**

- Decomposition analysis helped identify regional differences.
- Heatmaps revealed periods with severe congestion.

### Dashboard Preview

![Page 2](dashboard%20images/Page%202.png)

# 👨‍⚕️ Page 3 – Workforce Capacity & Outcomes

## Business Focus

Evaluate staffing efficiency and understand burnout risks.

### Key Insights

👨‍⚕️ **Staffing Capacity**

- Departments such as Orthopaedics and Oncology required higher nursing support.

⚠ **Burnout Risk**

- Hospitals with higher overtime exhibited increased burnout scores.

📊 **Workforce Health**

- Several hospitals were categorized as medium priority, highlighting opportunities for workload optimization.
