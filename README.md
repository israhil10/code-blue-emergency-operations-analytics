# 📌 Project Overview

**Code Blue – Emergency Operations & Patient Flow Analytics** is a three-page Power BI dashboard developed for **FP20 Analytics Challenge #38**.

The project focuses on understanding emergency department performance, patient flow bottlenecks, workforce capacity, and healthcare outcomes to support operational efficiency and improve patient experience.

The dashboard transforms healthcare data into actionable insights that enable leaders to optimize resources, reduce delays, and enhance operational readiness.

---

# 🚑 Business Problem

Emergency departments operate under constant pressure from:

- Rising patient demand
- Treatment delays
- Capacity constraints
- Workforce burnout
- Resource allocation challenges

Without effective analytics, healthcare leaders struggle to identify bottlenecks and optimize patient throughput.

This project addresses these challenges through interactive dashboards that provide visibility into patient flow, operational performance, and workforce efficiency.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

✔ Analyze emergency department activity

✔ Identify patient flow bottlenecks

✔ Evaluate admission trends and waiting times

✔ Understand diagnosis and severity patterns

✔ Monitor workforce utilization and burnout risks

✔ Improve operational readiness and patient outcomes

# 📈 Key Insights

### Emergency Operations

- Diagnosis categories driving the highest ED demand were identified.
- Patient outcomes and severity distribution were analyzed.
- Regional variations in patient volumes were explored.

### Patient Flow

- Peak congestion periods were highlighted.
- Delay drivers and bottlenecks were identified.
- Waiting times and length of stay patterns were analyzed.

### Workforce Analytics

- Staffing efficiency across hospitals was evaluated.
- Burnout risks associated with overtime were identified.
- Workforce health scores supported operational decision-making.
