# Project Overview

Code Blue – Emergency Operations & Patient Flow Analytics is a three-page Power BI dashboard developed for FP20 Analytics Challenge #38.

The project focuses on understanding emergency department performance, patient flow bottlenecks, workforce capacity, and healthcare outcomes to support operational efficiency and improve patient experience.

---

# Business Problem

Emergency departments operate under increasing pressure due to:

- Rising patient demand
- Treatment delays
- Capacity constraints
- Workforce burnout
- Resource allocation challenges

Without effective analytics, healthcare leaders struggle to identify bottlenecks and optimize patient throughput.

---

# Objectives

- Analyze emergency department activity.
- Identify patient flow bottlenecks.
- Evaluate waiting times and admission trends.
- Understand diagnosis and severity patterns.
- Monitor workforce utilization and burnout risks.
- Improve patient outcomes and operational readiness.

# Key Insights

### Emergency Operations

- Diagnosis categories driving the highest ED demand were identified.
- Severity mix and patient outcomes provided insight into care quality.

### Patient Flow

- Peak congestion periods and bottlenecks were highlighted.
- Waiting time patterns revealed opportunities for improvement.

### Workforce Analytics

- Staffing efficiency was evaluated across hospitals.
- Burnout risks associated with overtime were identified.
